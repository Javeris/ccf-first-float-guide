What is floating

60 minutes of complete stillness.

Floating is simple. You lie back in warm saltwater and you don't do anything. The water is skin temperature. The room is dark. It's quiet.

Six hundred kilograms of dissolved Epsom salt makes the water so dense your body floats effortlessly — no kicking, no effort, no tension.

We've been delivering float therapy to Central Coast locals for over 10 years. There's nothing to learn.

Inside the pod

Bigger than you expect.

Roughly the size of a small car — tall enough to sit up in, wide enough to spread your arms.

You don't have to close the lid. You are in complete control from the moment you step in.

The sensation

What the water feels like.

Skin temperature. After a few minutes, you stop noticing where your body ends and the water begins.

The salt holds you up completely. No kicking, no bracing. You just exist.

Most people stop noticing the water entirely. What's left is stillness.

The mental shift

What happens to your mind.

Not all at once. First the noise softens. Then the to-do list fades.

Then there's just quiet. Your brain stops scanning for threats. It trusts the stillness.

Researchers call it the theta state — the same deep rest that happens just before sleep.

Before you arrive

A few practical things.

No experience needed. Just a handful of things that make your first float even better.

1

Skip the razor

Don't shave or wax in the 12 hours before. The salt can sting.

2

Eat lightly beforehand

A light meal 1–2 hours before is ideal.

3

Arrive 10 minutes early

We'll walk you through everything on your first visit.

4

We provide everything

Towels, earplugs, body wash, shampoo — all included.

5

Wear nothing inside

Each pod is in its own private room. Most people float without a swimsuit.

6

Nothing to learn

Don't meditate, don't try. Just get in and let go.

Common questions

What people ask us.

Do I need to be able to swim?

Not at all. The salt makes it so buoyant that floating is completely effortless.

What do I wear inside the pod?

Nothing. Each pod is in its own completely private room with a shower.

What if I fall asleep?

Very common and completely safe. The buoyancy keeps you floating on your back regardless.

Is the water clean?

Yes — extremely. Filtered and treated with UV and hydrogen peroxide between every float.

How long is a session?

60 minutes standard. 90-minute sessions also available.

Can I float if I'm pregnant?

Many pregnant women find it deeply relieving. Check with your doctor first.
